Born in the Dark, Built for the Light

It started in 2019 with three people, two laptops, and a whiteboard that read: "What if we actually fixed it instead of just patching?" CEO Vera Hargrove had just survived a catastrophic breach at a Fortune 100 bank. CTO Marcus Webb had spent a decade at NSA. And lead researcher Soo-Jin Kang had found more zero-days before breakfast than most firms find in a quarter.

They named the company NullCastle — because null is where all exceptions are handled, and a castle is what you build when you're serious about keeping enemies out.

Today we operate from five global nodes, serve 214 enterprise clients, and maintain a threat intelligence database of over 2.4 million indicators of compromise. But the whiteboard is still there. And the question hasn't changed.

The Protocols We Live By

  • [ 0x01 ]
    Radical Transparency

    We share threat intelligence, publish incident post-mortems, and never hide behind NDA theatre. If we messed up, you hear it from us first.

  • [ 0x02 ]
    Adversarial Thinking

    We model our defences by thinking like attackers. This isn't a buzzword. It means our engineers maintain active CTF records and real-world red team credentials.

  • [ 0x03 ]
    Zero Complacency

    Complacency is a vulnerability. We run internal red-team exercises every 30 days. Our own systems. Our own assumptions. Constantly challenged.

  • [ 0x04 ]
    Client Obsession

    Not in the free-pizza sense. In the 3am-incident-call, skip-the-boardroom-and-talk-to-the-engineer sense. Your problem is our problem.

  • [ 0x05 ]
    Knowledge Sharing

    Our research is public. Our CVEs are responsibly disclosed. The security community made us — we make the community stronger in return.

  • [ 0x06 ]
    Operate with Honour

    We've turned down lucrative contracts that crossed ethical lines. There's always another client. There's only one reputation.

  • [ 0x07 ]
    The Long View

    Security is a practice, not a product. We build relationships measured in years, not projects. Your threat landscape in 2030 is already our problem today.

  • [ 0x08 ]
    No Drama Policy

    Crisis brings enough drama on its own. Inside NullCastle: calm, clear, decisive. Think Saul Goodman's confidence without the felony charges.
